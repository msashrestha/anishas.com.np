Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Anisha’s Musings, which is available at the URL https://anishas.com.np, your privacy is very important. To better protect your privacy, the website has provided this statement explaining the information practices that are followed and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected, used and disclosed. To make this statement easy to find, it has been made available on the main menu of the website.

The Information We Collect

Anisha’s Musings is a static website that does not track, collect, store any information, or set any cookies. There is no account on the website itself.

Here are some third party services that are used for storage, propagation and security of data:

  • GitHub Pages is where the website is hosted. Please refer to GitHub’s privacy practices document for details.
  • The website is stored in git, on GitHub. The git software does collect names and email addresses in repositories. GitHub has the account information. Please refer to GitHub’s privacy practices document for details.
  • For uniform representation of fonts, this website uses web fonts provided by Google. When you open a page, your browser loads the required web fonts into your browser cache to display texts and fonts correctly. For this purpose, your browser has to establish a direct connection to Google servers. Thus, Google becomes aware that the website was accessed via your IP address. The use of Google Fonts is done in the interest of a uniform and attractive presentation of the website. This constitutes a justified interest pursuant to Art. 6 (1) (f) DSGVO. If your browser does not support web fonts, a standard font is used by your computer. Further information about handling of user data can be found at Google Font’s Frequently Asked Questions page and in Google’s privacy policy.
  • YouTube videos may be embedded in some pages on the website. YouTube embeds are rewritten as to not use cookies. Please refer to YouTube’s Privacy Guidelines for details.
  • To enhance server security and speed, this website uses the CDN services from CloudFlare. For this purpose, your browser connects trough CloudFlare CDN to where the website is hosted, which means that CloudFlare processes all data that is sent from or to this website. The use of CloudFlare is done in the interest of a secure and fast accessibility of the website. This constitutes a justified interest pursuant to Art. 6 (1) (f) DSGVO. More information on CloudFlare’s GDPR policy can be found here.

Our Commitment to Data Security

This website uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology, which encrypts data to and from our website.

About Links to Other Sites

This site may contain links to other sites. The Anisha’s Musings website does not control the information collection on sites that can be reached through those links. If you have questions about the data collection procedures of the linked sites, please contact these sites directly.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Anisha’s Musings website reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any changes are made, the revised Privacy Policy will also be posted on the same link on the website.

Contact Information
