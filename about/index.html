About

I am Anisha and I am passionate about putting my thoughts and emotions into words. I believe in kindness and sympathy towards all and try to emulate the same in my daily life. I want my writing to entertain as well as empower, provoke and inspire, or in the least help someone in their time of need.

Here you will find poetry, tidbits about my life and experiences, some serious writing, and sometimes the not so serious stuff as well. But, rest assured, you can view my progress in life in words, and go with me in my journey of learning and new experiences.

Peace and grace.
